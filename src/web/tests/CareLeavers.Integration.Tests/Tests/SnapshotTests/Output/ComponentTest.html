When you leave care

You do not have to leave care leave care until you’re 18.

If you want to, you can leave care at 16 or 17. This is a decision that your social worker can help you with.

Before you leave care

You'll get support to start preparing early so that you feel more ready when you leave. This might start when you’re 15, 16, or 17, depending on when you leave.

Your social worker will help you plan your move out of care, or you may get a personal advisor to support you.

After you leave care

Your local council must support you until you’re 21, but there is support you can get until you’re 25.
